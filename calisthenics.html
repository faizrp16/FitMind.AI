Progression Tips

  • Increase Reps First: Master 15-20 reps at your current level before progressing
  • Perfect Form Matters: Quality over quantity. Bad form = fewer gains + injury risk
  • Add Difficulty Gradually: One step at a time. Don't jump multiple levels
  • Track Your Progress: Keep a log of reps and sets to monitor improvement
  • Recovery is Key: Rest days allow muscles to repair and grow stronger
